# The Essence of Competition Is Avoiding Competition

Everyone believes: Business is about fighting in red oceans and winning the competition.

But the truth is: Smart players avoid competition from the start. Monopoly is the endgame.

Why?

Peter Thiel shocked Silicon Valley in "Zero to One": "Competition is for losers." Google owns 90% of search with 29% profit margins; airlines compete brutally for 3% margins.
