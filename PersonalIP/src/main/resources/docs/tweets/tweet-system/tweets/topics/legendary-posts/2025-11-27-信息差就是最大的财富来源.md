# Information Asymmetry Is the Biggest Source of Wealth

On the surface: Stock trading, real estate brokerage, headhunting, cross-border e-commerce seem different

But fundamentally they are: Capturing value during the journey from information asymmetry to symmetry

**Evidence Chain:**

Evidence 1: US Real Estate Commission Data (1995-2024)
- 1995: Average 6% commission, high information asymmetry (buyers could only access listings through agents)
- 2024: After Zillow/Redfin platforms emerged, commission dropped to 2.5-3%, 58% decline
- Critical turning point: 2006 Zillow launch, within 2 years agent income dropped $12B/year
- Conclusion: Speed of information gap closing = speed of profit disappearing

Evidence 2: HFT Arbitrage revealed in Michael Lewis's "Flash Boys" (2010-2014)
- High-frequency trading firms exploited fiber optic transmission speed differences for NY-Chicago arbitrage
- Information time gap: 3 milliseconds
- Annual profit: $20B (entire HFT industry)
- After 2014 IEX "speed bump exchanges" appeared, this arbitrage space shrunk 90%
- Insight: Even 3 milliseconds of information gap was worth $20 billion

Evidence 3: Personal Cross-Border Arbitrage Case (2019-2022)
- Found electronic component: China price $12, US Amazon $45, information gap $33
- Operation: Direct purchase from Shenzhen, sell via Amazon FBA
- Data: 2019 profit margin 73%, 2020 46%, 2021 28%, 2022 12%
- Cause: More people discovered this gap, competition flattened prices
- Lifecycle: From discovery to dividend disappearance, only 36 months

**Inference:**

Once you understand this essence, you'll realize:

All excess profits are temporary because information gaps always get filled. The only moat is:
1. Speed of creating information gaps > speed of others discovering
2. Or having other barriers after information symmetry (brand, network effects, economies of scale)

**Core Insights:**

**Underlying Logic:**

Nassim Taleb in "Antifragile": "Information wants to be free, but wealth comes from delaying that freedom."

All business models fundamentally answer one question: How do you capture value before information symmetry?
