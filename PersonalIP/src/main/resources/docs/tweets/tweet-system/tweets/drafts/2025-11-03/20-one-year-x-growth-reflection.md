7/ 小众 > 大众

8/ 长期主义
   building audience是marathon
   别看一周的数据

9/ give > take
   先提供价值

10/ enjoy the process

## Tweet Content (English Version)

1 year of intentional sharing on X.

From 300 to 1.3K followers (goal: 2K by year-end).

Not explosive growth, but learned a ton.

10 most important lessons:

1/ Quality > Quantity
   Went from 8 posts/day to 3-4
   Engagement rate doubled

2/ Authentic > Perfect
   Posts about failures often perform better than successes

3/ Consistency > Viral
   Steady publishing > Occasional viral hit

4/ Replies > Posts
   Spend 50% of time in comments
   Build real connections

5/ Professional + Personal
   Pure tech = boring
   Pure personal = shallow
   Mix = depth

6/ Metrics lie
   10K impressions ≠ impact
   10 deep conversations > 100 likes

7/ Niche > Broad
   1K real fans in vertical > 10K zombie followers

8/ Long-term thinking
   Building audience is a marathon
   Don't judge by one week

9/ Give > Take
   Provide value first
   Returns will follow

10/ Enjoy the process
    If you don't enjoy sharing
    You won't sustain it

Next year goals:
→ 2K → 5K
→ Open source 2 projects
→ Help 100 people

Thank you to everyone who engages 🙏

What's been your biggest takeaway on X?
