## 推文内容 (中文版)

我花了6年读计算机博士，学到的最重要的一课不是算法。

是"如何在不确定中做决策"。

学术界：花6个月验证一个假设可能是错的
工业界：花6天构建一个功能可能没人用
交易：花6秒做决定可能损失6位数

唯一不变的：
用数据说话 + 快速迭代 + 接受失败

Ph.D.教会我的不是完美，而是在不完美中前进。

## Tweet Content (English Version)

I spent 6 years earning my CS Ph.D., and the most important lesson wasn't about algorithms.

It was "how to make decisions under uncertainty."

Academia: 6 months to validate a hypothesis that might be wrong
Industry: 6 days to build a feature nobody might use
Trading: 6 seconds to make a decision that could lose 6 figures

The constant:
Data-driven + rapid iteration + accepting failure

Ph.D. didn't teach me perfection. It taught me to move forward despite imperfection.
