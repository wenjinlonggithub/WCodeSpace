# Compound Thinking: The Eighth Wonder of the World

Conventional wisdom tells us: work hard, move fast, see immediate results.

But the truth might be the exact opposite. The greatest wealth, deepest wisdom, and strongest capabilities all come from "getting rich slowly."

**Why?**

Albert Einstein said: "Compound interest is the eighth wonder of the world. He who understands it, earns it; he who doesn't, pays it."

Observation 1: $1 at 10% annual return becomes $1.10 after 1 year (earn 10 cents). After 50 years = $117.39 (117x return). Time transforms 10% into 11,739%. This is the magic of compounding.
