# How Retail Investors Survive Bear Markets

Common retail investor mistakes:

Mistake 2: Re-entering immediately after stop-loss triggers
Set stop-loss at -15%, got triggered, then bought back next day seeing a 3% bounce. Trapped in "sell low-buy high" loop with 12% monthly transaction costs.

Mistake 3: Using living expenses to buy the dip, losing patience
Used emergency fund to buy in March 2020, forced to sell in April when rent was due, missing the subsequent 42% rally over 5 months.
